Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna is some form of nourishment commonly consumed by humans. Especially in the United States. Lasagna seemed to be a mix of thinly sliced sheets of noodle stuff mixed in with variations of the tomatoe, some meat, and an unhealthy amount of cheese.

Ingredients

  • Italian Sausage
  • Lean Ground Beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Crushed Tomatoes
  • Canned Tomatoe Sauce
  • Canned Tomatoe Paste
  • Sugar
  • Parsley
  • Dried Basil Leaves
  • Salt
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Fennel Seeds
  • Black Pepper
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Egg

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Cook the meats, onion, and garlic in an Dutch oven until browned
  3. Stir in all the tomatoe variants, water, and all the seasonings
  4. Simmer for 1.5 hours
  5. Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water for 8-10 minutes
  6. Mix cheese, egg, parsley, and salt
  7. Put meat sauce in the bottom of a baking dish, add noodle, then cheese, then more cheese, some meat sauce, and to top of off, add more cheese.
  8. Repeat for more layers
  9. Bake at 375 degrees while covered in tin foil for 25 minutes
  10. Remove tin foil and bake for another 25 minutes
