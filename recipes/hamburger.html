Hamburger Recipe

Description

The hamburger is a traditional cuisine of the United States, yet it is commonly consumed across the world. The hamburger is a sandwich, but the meat is thinker than a traditional sandwich, and more items are added, making the hamburger a highly vertical consumable.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Hamburger Patties
  • Lettuce
  • Sliced Tomatoes
  • Ketchup
  • Pickles

Directions

  1. Take out two pieces of bread
  2. Put on every other ingredient on top of one slice of bread, order does not matter
  3. Put the second slice of bread on top
