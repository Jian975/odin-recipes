Pizza Recipe

Description

The Pizza is a common staple in the United States. Originally created in Italy, the pizza has became one of the most iconic food items of the Americans. The pizza has a circular slice of bread at its bottom, tomatoe sauce on top of that bread, and finally a heavy layer of cheese to top it off. The pizza can be customized by adding any number of "toppings" on top of the cheese.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Tomatoe Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Diced Pineapples

Directions

  1. Put tomatoe sauce on top of bread
  2. Put cheese on top of tomatoe sauce
  3. Put pineapples on top of cheese
  4. Bake until cooked
